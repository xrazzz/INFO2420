Web Site Critiques

Trader Joe's - http://www.traderjoes.com/

  1. What was your first impression of this site?

    My first impression of this site is the simplicity of it. They made it feel relaxed and easy to navigate.

  2. How does this site organize the flow of information?

    This site organizes the flow of information by sectioning the site off into different sections based on the food item. They made it clean and straightforward.

  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you?

    The site structure makes sense to me. Again, the simplicity of the website makes it easy to navigate.

  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it?

    Their graphics, layout, and multimedia enhance their site. They include different recipes on the home page, high-quality photos that aren't too colorful, and they have cohesive branding throughout the site.

  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?

    The load time only took a second or two; it was reasonable for the site due to the simplicity.

  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?

    The text color and size were reasonable; it wasn't too big or too small. The font and text color made it easily readable. The image used added value to the site instead of detracting from it.

  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?

    The site did have a navigation drop-down list, and it was well done. They sectioned it off by food category, which makes it easy for visitors to find a specific item.

  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?

    Yes, their color scheme was consistent across all of their pages; their branding was on point.

  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?

    When shrinking the window size, the site responded well to it and adjusted to the sizing.

  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

    This site offers a home page that was very well done. They include everything someone might be looking for on their home page, while also keeping it clean.
